ABOUT

Edwin Tran is a multidisciplinary designer based in Toronto, ON. Placing a strong emphasis on research and storytelling, Edwin is interested in exploring themes within the arts and culture using a media-agnostic approach.

Graduated with a B.A. in Art History from UCLA, Edwin wrote his undergraduate thesis on the phenomenology and user experience of early modern Italian prints/books with interactive components.
