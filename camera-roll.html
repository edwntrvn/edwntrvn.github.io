ABOUT

Edwin Tran is a graphic designer and artist from Toronto/Paris based in Los Angeles. Placing a strong emphasis on research and storytelling, Edwin is interested in exploring themes within the arts and culture using a media-agnostic approach.

Currently pursuing a BA in Art History at UCLA, Edwin is writing his undergraduate thesis on the phenomenology and user experience of early modern Italian prints/books.
